campaign design

InMotion campaigns are always rooted in primary objectives. We craft bullet-proof strategies that are dynamic and flexible. Our creative teams incorporate split tests so that we can understand how variations in key factors affect outcomes. Power in planning, success through strategy.

copy/creative

We recognize that not all users, distribution channels, and goals are the same. We adjust messaging and positioning to resonate with individual audiences while maintaining a uniform brand voice. The cohesion between copy and creative is essential in guiding interactions that convert.

reporting

Every campaign is like its own scientific experiment -- it all comes down to the data. Interpreting data to recognize audience patterns can drastically increase conversions. Through integrations with third-party tracking tools, we compile data, discover insights, and evaluate campaign effectiveness.

optimization

Throughout a campaigns lifetime, we constantly monitor data, isolate variables, and identify top-performing variants. Our team works quickly to capitalize on data insights to further improve the desired conversion and ensure optimal return on ad spend.
