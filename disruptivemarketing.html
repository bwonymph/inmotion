what is augmented reality?

Augmented reality (AR) is a form of technology that superimposes a computer-generated graphic onto a user’s view of the real world. One of the most popular examples of AR technology is inside of the game: Pokémon Go.

3d modeling/animation

Projects begin with a concept and are then translated to 3D models, which come to life as AR lenses. These AR objects can be animated, interactive and even have some intelligence. The possibilities are truly endless.

applications

To casual observers, applications of AR might look trivial but the viral results speak for themselves. When people use AR to create content and socialize, they’re enhancing natural behaviors; we can harness those behaviors and use them as a marketing tool. Augmented Reality presents a unique viral marketing opportunity with tremendous upside as its potential is almost completely untapped by most businesses. We firmly believe that AR has a place in all customer-facing industries.

case study

We created an AR lens in 24 hours and distributed it to 32 of our Snapchat followers. As you can see, in less than a day, the lens got 2,800 views. This is about an 87 view to 1 subscriber ratio. If we had a list of 1,000+ subscribers that would mean 87,000+ views. As AR technology becomes more popular, we continue to pioneer Snapchat and Facebook strategies for businesses.
